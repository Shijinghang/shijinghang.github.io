I am Shijinghang. My work focuses on astronomy, data-driven scientific discovery, and AI-assisted research workflows.

I am especially interested in building reliable tools that combine astronomical evidence, source-level measurements, and careful reasoning. This website collects my research notes, publications, projects, and academic materials.

Research Interests
======

- Quasar candidate auditing and source-level evidence workflows
- Multimodal and agentic AI systems for scientific reasoning
- Machine learning applications in astronomy
